Terms & Conditions

1. About Incisio

Incisio is a digital procedure logbook for Surgical First Assistants (SFAs), Scrub Nurses, ODPs, and other non-medical theatre staff at Practice Plus Group Hospital, Ilford. It supports professional portfolio development and personal record-keeping.

2. Not a Clinical Record

Important: Incisio is not a clinical system and does not form part of any patient record. Do not record patient names, NHS numbers, dates of birth, or any information that could identify an individual. All entries must relate to your own professional development only.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in the cloud (Supabase) and is associated with your account. It is also cached locally on your device for offline use. You can export your data at any time via Settings → Export.

Data is not lost if you clear your browser or close the app — it is backed up to the cloud automatically whenever you are online.

4. Smart Entry & AI

The Smart Entry (✨) feature sends the text you type to an external AI service (Anthropic). Never type patient-identifiable information into the Smart Entry box.

5. No Patient Data

You must not enter any patient-identifiable information anywhere in the app. Entering patient data constitutes a potential data protection breach under UK GDPR.

6. Professional Use

Entries should accurately reflect procedures you personally participated in. Falsification of logbook entries may constitute professional misconduct and could be referred to your regulatory body (NMC, HCPC, or FPA).

7. Accuracy of Information

Procedure codes (OPCS-4 / CPT) and clinical information are provided as guidance only. Verify all codes before formal submission.

8. Acceptance

By continuing you confirm you have read these terms, will not enter patient data, and understand your responsibilities as a registered healthcare professional.

User Guide

Incisio v2.1 — cloud-synced surgical logbook. Your data is stored securely in the cloud and cached offline on your device.

Getting Started

1
Create an accountSign up with your email and password. Each person has their own private logbook.
2
Set up your profileAdd your role, registration number, and hospital in Settings. These auto-fill every new entry.
3
Log a procedureTap the + button. Date and procedure name are required. All other fields are optional but recommended.
4
Use Smart Entry ✨Type a natural description like "lap chole with Mr Smith, assisted, 45 min" and tap ✨. AI will populate the fields. Never type patient information here.

Sync & Offline Use

The app works fully offline — entries save to your device instantly. When back online they sync to the cloud automatically. The dot in the top-right shows sync status: ● synced · ● pending · ● offline.

Logging a Procedure

Procedure Name — start typing to see OPCS/CPT suggestions. Tap to auto-fill code and specialty.

Competency Level — Observed, Assisted, Performed (Supervised), or Performed (Independent).

Complications — the app auto-suggests a Clavien-Dindo grade as you type.

Supervisor Sign-off — hand your device to your supervisor to trace their signature with a finger.

Managing Entries

Tap any entry to view full details, edit, or duplicate. Swipe left to delete — you'll have a few seconds to undo. Use the search bar or specialty chips to filter your log.

Exporting

Settings → Download PDF generates a formatted logbook with cover page, all entries, and embedded signatures. Settings → Download CSV gives you a spreadsheet backup.

Privacy & AI

Your logbook is private — only you can access it. The Smart Entry ✨ feature sends text to an AI service. Never type patient names, NHS numbers, or any identifying information anywhere in the app.
